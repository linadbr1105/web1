내가 추구하는 가치

I think this society is sick.
There are so many Boasting people and so many people who feel a sense of relative deprivation either.
At the same time, they would considers themselves worthless creature.
They hates their own lives looking at very few people who live splendid lives.
Actually, I did.

But I decide to love my life from now on.
Life is a continuous of pain and anxiety, and the flashy moments are literally "moments"
In other words, we have to accept that there is no reason why our lives are always bright and enjoyable.
If we fail to do so, we will keep to hate our lives during the dark times that make up most of our lives.
We must love even painful moments.
